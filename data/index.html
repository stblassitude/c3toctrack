APIs for the train tracker at #cccamp23

See c3toc.de and github.com/stblassitude/c3toctrack for more information.

  • tracks.json: information about the tracks, in custom format
  • tracks-geo.json: information about the tracks, in GeoJson format
  • trains.json: information about the trains, in particular, their current position, custom format
  • trains-geo.json: information about the trains, in GeoJson format
